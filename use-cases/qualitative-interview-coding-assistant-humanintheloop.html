Qualitative interview coding assistant (human‑in‑the‑loop)

Generate candidate codes and theme suggestions; researchers validate and refine codebooks.

Social Sciences Analysis qualitativecodingthemes

Workflow

  1. De-identify transcripts.
  2. Generate candidate codes using an approved model or local pipeline.
  3. Researchers refine the codebook and re-code segments.
  4. Audit a sample for consistency and bias.

Recommended tools

  • Whisper (local transcription) (Approval: Conditional; Class: Sensitive)
  • JupyterLab (Approval: Approved; Class: Restricted)

Governance notes

Transcripts often contain PII; treat as Sensitive/Restricted; keep audit trails and ensure approved storage.
